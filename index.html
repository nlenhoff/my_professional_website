About Me

I am an Embedded Firmware and Software Engineer at Jacobs Solutions Inc. I also attend the University of Maryland, Baltimore County as a M.S Computer Engineering Student. I obtained my B.S Electrical Engineering at the University of Maryland, College Park in spring of 2022. My minor was in Spanish Language, Culture, and Professional Contexts and I received an Honors Citation for the Advanced Cybersecurity Experience for Students (ACES) program.

Focus Areas

Industry
At Jacobs, I work with embedded systems and software defined radios (SDR). My specialization is firmware and software applications utilized for the operation and development of custom communication hardware. The hardware utilized in our designs often includes microcontrollers, FPGAs, SoCs, RFICs, and more.

Research
In academia, my research focuses on embedded systems and the development of small footprint and low power designs for industrial and mission critical solutions. The following topics are my specific areas of interest:

  • Microelectronics
  • Sensor Fusion
  • Pedestrian Dead Reckoning
  • Simultaneous Localization and Mapping
  • Data Visualization and Communication
  • Wearable Technology
